Workforce Training Non-profit

Are you looking to diversify your funding streams? Are you looking for ways to train young adults on local projects? Build United will help you find more opportunities for hands-on, project-based learning in your local community. By engaging with the Build United platform, you will have access to a database of organizations seeking local talent and projects taking place in the upcoming year.

Talent opportunities posted include project timelines and skills needed, connecting you directly with the local work. This service is free to use and provides the essential opportunities your trainees need to grow their skill sets and be a part of the change taking place in their community. You will have insight into the pipeline of work needed in the upcoming year and the ability to build your training programming based on those needs.
