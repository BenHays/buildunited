About

At Build United, our vision is to turn gentrification into integration. Our mission is to promote social responsibility and sustainable workforces by advancing partnerships among local residents, workforce training non-profits, and business developers.

Overview

Build United is a job bidding platform that:

Seamlessly builds digital connections between workforce training non-profits and business developers

Establishes a baseline for local community responsibility through “Partner Points”
